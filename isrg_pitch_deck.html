Risk Assessment

Bear Case Rebuttal

Slide 11 / 15
Risk 1: Valuation — 57.5x Trailing PE
Forward PE calculated live from consensus EPS ~$12.06. At $452.07, forward PE = 37.5x on accelerating earnings growth. 75–81% recurring revenue compounders at 17–20% CAGR command premium multiples. Three unpriced catalysts make the forward multiple conservative, not stretched.
MANAGEABLE
Risk 2: Gross Margin Compression — -146bp FY2025
Management guided 67–68% gross margin for FY2026 on January 22 earnings call. Da Vinci Xi (2014) compressed margins for 6–8 quarters then recovered and expanded. DV5 is quarter 3–6 of that identical cycle. FY2025 was peak compression. Recovery confirmed and already guided.
↑ Management guided 67–68% FY2026 (Jan 22, 2026 earnings call)
SELF-RESOLVING — Xi Precedent Confirmed by Management
Risk 3: Competition — Hugo, Versius, Ottava
CMR Surgical raised $1.43B to generate $48.7M revenue (PitchBook). Momentis last round $14.3M November 2025 (PitchBook). J&J Ottava delayed to 2027+. 17M kinematic sequences irreplicable. Surgeon retraining requires 100+ supervised procedures. Competitors expand the TAM — they do not take share from a surgeon who has performed 500 da Vinci procedures.
MARKET EXPANDER
Risk 4: China Tariff Exposure
China represents less than 5% of ISRG revenue. US manufacturing base insulates from retaliatory tariff risk. Europe direct acquisition simultaneously reduces geographic concentration and improves diversification.
LIMITED
Risk 5: Hospital Capex Budget Pressure
28% of Q4 2025 placements are already on usage-based leases. The bear case objection — hospitals cannot afford $1.5–2.5M upfront — is simultaneously ISRG's product solution. The bear case is self-defeating. 23x ASC TAM expansion available today.
SELF-SOLVING
Risk 6: Ion Platform Cannibalization Risk
Ion targets the $3B bronchoscopy TAM with zero procedural overlap with da Vinci. Proprietary single-use catheters replicate the toll-road model in a separate anatomical category. ~51% procedure growth FY2025. Ion is additive revenue, not redistributed revenue.
ADDITIVE
