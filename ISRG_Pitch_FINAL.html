SLIDE 6  ·  BUSINESS MODEL EVOLUTION

ASC + Per-Procedure Pricing

Usage-Based Leasing

28%

of Q4 2025 placements were usage-based or operating leases. Hospitals pay per procedure — converting capex to opex and deepening the toll-road model.

ASC Migration

Ambulatory Surgery Centers represent a massive expansion of addressable sites. ASCs are cost-sensitive — the usage-based model removes the $1.5–2.5M upfront barrier, enabling 2–3x the addressable market.

Higher Utilization = More Tolls

Usage-based pricing aligns ISRG incentives with hospitals. More procedures per system = more I&A revenue. Hospitals push utilization to reduce effective per-case cost. ISRG wins either way.
